About Levanter (바람)

Levanter (바람; "Baram" meaning "Wind") is the third track and title track from Stray Kids' fifth mini album Clé : LEVANTER, released on December 9, 2019.

🎵 Composition

The title track "Levanter" was written by JYP head Park Jin Young and musician Herz Analog, along with the team's production group 3RACHA.

All I needed was me, I walked where my feet took me, I felt the light pouring in the moment I left the tunnel that kept me in my dreams

The song is a power ballad with a length of 3:15.

📅 Album Information

  • Album: Clé : LEVANTER (5th Mini Album)
  • Released: December 9, 2019
  • Genre: Power ballad
  • Length: 03:15
  • Label: JYP Entertainment

🎬 Music Video & Choreography

  • Music Video Director: Kim Ji-yong
  • Choreography: Na Tae-hoon, Sim Kyu-jin, MOTF, Choi Young-jun, RyuD

🏆 Achievements

  • "Levanter" earned a music show win on M!Countdown on December 19, 2019.

📝 Trivia

  • Levanters are strong easterly winds in the Mediterranean region.
  • The album was originally set to be released on November 25, 2019, but was delayed due to Woojin's departure from the group on October 27, 2019.
  • The album was re-recorded and finally released on December 9, 2019.
  • The group held its ninth mini fanmeeting after the pre-recording of "Levanter" on Show! Music Core on December 14, 2019.
